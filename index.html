About Us

The Pre-Endura 2k18 is going to be held on 6-7th oct-2018 in the college premises. Pre-Endura is a intra college sports fest of miet. It is a great platform to showcase your talent so as to get chance to represent your college team in Endura which is a inter college sports fest.

Following are the games which will be played in Pre-Endura:-
  • Basketball
  • Volleyball
  • Football
  • Badminton
  • Snooker
  • Table Tennis
  • Chess
  • Carrom
All the students of college are requested to grace the occasion.

TIMING :- 10:00 AM ONWARDS
